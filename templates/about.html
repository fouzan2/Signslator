About Us

Welcome to our American Sign Language Detection Website, SignsLator!

SignsLator was the product of a project under the supervision of Dr. Prof. Asad Raza Kazmi. SignsLator would not have been here today without the incredible support we received from Dr. Prof. Asad Raza Kazmi!

At SignsLator, we are a dynamic and diverse team of Two passionate individuals dedicated to the fields of data science and machine learning. Our mission is to break barriers and bridge gaps through technology, making information and communication accessible to everyone, including those who use sign languages.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where language is no longer a barrier. We believe that technology has the power to revolutionize the way we communicate and interact, and we are committed to harnessing that potential to empower individuals from all walks of life.
